###### **Do’s of Social Media**

1. Think before you post
2. Ensure your content is appropriate and respectful.
3. Use strong privacy settings
4. Control who can access your personal information.
5. Verify information before sharing



###### **Don’ts of Social Media**

1. Don’t share personal details publicly
2. Avoid posting sensitive information like phone numbers or addresses.
3. Don’t engage in cyberbullying
4. Do not harass, insult, or threaten others.
5. Don’t click suspicious links
